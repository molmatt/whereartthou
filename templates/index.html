Where Art Thou

A Web App to Help You Decide Which Art Museum You Would Prefer









Every day, 'Where Art Thou' pulls the last ten images from the twitter feeds of art museums in New York City.
It then runs these images through a convolutional neural network to filter out images that are not art. While
the filtering process is not perfect, it accomplishes the task with 89% accuracy. That being said, if you do see
anything you wouldn't classify as art, feel free to skip rating that image. It won't factor in to the suggestion.

Have fun!
